About White Water Rafting Company

Founded in 2005, White Water Rafting Company has been dedicated to providing the most thrilling and safe rafting experiences on the best rivers in California and beyond. Our experienced guides and well-maintained equipment ensure a fun adventure for all skill levels.

We believe in preserving nature and respecting our waterways, so we partner with local conservation groups and practice eco-friendly rafting techniques.

Whether you're seeking a family outing or an adrenaline-pumping trip, join us and explore the wild rivers with confidence and excitement!
